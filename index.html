Mandatory Aadhaar Photos

When you click submit, your email application will open. You MUST attach clear photos of the following three documents in your email:

  • Your own (Student's) Aadhaar Card photo
  • Your Mother's Aadhaar Card photo
  • Your Father's Aadhaar Card photo

Payment instructions

The payment QR Code will be provided in the email. You must scan and complete the payment of ₹700 directly through that QR code in the email thread. Your PEN will only be processed once both the payment and the 3 Aadhaar photos are submitted.
